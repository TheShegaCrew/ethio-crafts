## Design System Applied ✅

### Color Palette (3-4 colors max)
- ✅ **Deep Ochre** (#8B6F47) - Primary brand, trust signals
- ✅ **Warm Gold** (#D4A574) - Accents, secondary CTAs
- ✅ **Off-Black** (#1A1A1A) - Text, headings
- ✅ **White** (#FFFFFF) - All backgrounds

### Typography (2 fonts max)
- ✅ **Lora** (serif) - Headings (cultural elegance)
- ✅ **Geist** (sans-serif) - Body text (modern readability)
- ✅ **Proper Sizing** - H1: 28-32px, H2: 20-24px, body: 14-16px
- ✅ **Line Height** - 1.4-1.6 for readability

### Layout (Flexbox priority)
- ✅ **Flexbox** - Primary layout method
- ✅ **CSS Grid** - Complex 2D layouts (product grid)
- ✅ **Mobile First** - Responsive from 320px to 2560px
- ✅ **Breakpoints** - sm (640px), md (768px), lg (1024px), xl (1280px)
